What is FreeRider?

FreeRider is a Hong Kong carpooling community that connects drivers and passengers for smarter, cheaper, and more social rides.

Use it for daily commutes, airport trips, or splitting Taxi/Uber fares—coordinate pickup points in-app without sharing phone numbers.

4 ways to carpool

  • Drivers find passengers: Share petrol and tunnel costs.
  • Drivers find co-drivers: Take turns driving for long commutes.
  • Passengers find drivers: A more comfortable commute than crowded public transport.
  • Split Taxi/Uber: Ride together and cut costs.

How it works

  1. Post or search a trip: Set origin, destination, and time window.
  2. Find matches: Browse rides or wait for others to join.
  3. Chat in-app: Agree on pickup point, seats, and cost split.
  4. Go: Ride together and split the fare.
